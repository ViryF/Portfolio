Myself

Software Developer based out of Houston Texas.


I recently graduated from a Software Engineering Immersive bootcamp at General Assembly as a Full-stack Engineer. This portfolio will guide you through some of my projects. As you look around my portfolio, you will see some of the skills I've harnessed in a short period of time.


I am a Tenacious Full Stack Software Engineer with excellent problem solving and multitasking efficiency skills. I am detail-oriented, persistent, and adapt to changing environments while maintaining my focus. My background in customer service and problem-solving makes me focused on the end user.


My objective is to continue pushing my limits and learning in this path of my life and career. All while bringing in a new perspective and a sense of curiosity and creativity to my products in order to improve them.
